A+

If you are A+: You can give blood to A+ and AB+. You can receive blood from A+, A-, O+ and O-.

A-

If you are A-: You can give blood to A-, A+, AB- and AB+. You can receive blood from A- and O-.

B+

If you are B+: You can give blood to B+ and AB+. You can receive blood from B+, B-, O+ and O-.

B-

If you are B-: You can give blood to B-, B+, AB+ and AB-. You can receive blood from B- and O-.

AB+

People with AB+ blood can receive red blood cells from any blood type. AB+ can donate only to AB+.

AB-

AB- patients can receive red blood cells from all -ve blood types. AB- can give red blood cells to both AB- and AB+ blood types.

O+

Blood O+ can donate to A+, B+, AB+ and O+. Group O is not a universal donor.

O-

O- can donate to all Blood groups. People with O- blood can only receive red cell donations from O- donors.
